Questions

Do I need any technical knowledge?
None. You fill out an intake form, we build everything, and you get a dashboard and Telegram notifications. The most technical thing you do is click Approve or Reject on content cards.
Does the agent post things without my approval?
Never. Every email, post, newsletter, and comment goes through your approval dashboard before anything goes live. You stay in complete control of everything that represents your brand.
How does the agent learn my voice?
During setup we build a brand voice file from your existing content — past newsletters, posts, your intake form responses. The agent reads this before generating anything. Over time it gets sharper as you approve and reject content.

Currently: email outreach via Gmail, Beehiiv newsletter drafting, Stacker News, Nostr, Reddit, and social media research across X, LinkedIn, and Instagram. Additional platforms configured based on where your audience lives.
